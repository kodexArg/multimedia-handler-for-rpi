Welcome!

From this site you can control your fleet of multimedia devices RaspberryPi.

You can immediately start adding images as static videos from the Converter.

From the Videos on devices section you can copy and delete, that is, rearrange the videos on the enabled devices.

Remember that each device seeks to synchronize its videos every few minutes (usually 5'), so you need to wait at least that long for the Multimedia Signs to start playing a new uploaded video, or stop showing a delete clip.
